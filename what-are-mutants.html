What are Mutants?

Mutants encompass all sorts of creatures, including humans, that have undergone severe and mental physical changes after prolonged exposure to the Voidborne virus.

Almost unrecognizable from their original forms, mutants are living beings that have been transformed by the Voidborne virus. These highly dangerous creatures exhibit extreme predatory adaptations, including enhanced muscle mass, heightened prey drive, and an unrelenting craving for flesh. As mutants of the same species reproduce, they pass on even more terrifying traits to their offspring, such as sharp bone protrusions, larger canines, longer, tougher claws, and venomous glands. The latest generations of mutants are embodiments of pure destruction, capable of tearing through reinforced walls and withstanding entire clips of small-arms fire before going down.

More fearsome than their enhanced physical characteristics is their newfound hyper-aggression. Even what used to be placid herbivores have turned into rabid maneaters under the Voidborne affliction. Far from mindless, these creatures have been observed to employ pack tactics, ambushes, and bait to capture —and devour— their quarries. Mutants understandably see humans as prey, especially when they hold the advantage in numbers.

Warning:
Mutants retain no memory of their former selves. If you suspect that a person or creature has been exposed to the Voidborne virus, watch for early signs of infection. If symptoms are present, make sure to neutralize the threat before mutation sets in.
