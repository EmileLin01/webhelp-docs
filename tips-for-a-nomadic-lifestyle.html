Tips for a Nomadic Lifestyle

A short list of essential skills to help you live a non-sedentary life in a post-apocalyptic world.

As mutants started proliferating in the wilderness, most survivors were quick to seek refuge in the nearest well-fortified settlement or colony. Some, however, whether by choice or circumstance, went on to scrape a living beyond the relative safety of the walls. Today, pockets of nomads still roam the lands, relying entirely on their ingenuity and hardiness to stay alive in an increasingly hostile world.

If you are one of the few brave or unlucky enough to be sleeping under the stars, make sure to take these tips to heart. Failure to follow this advice will lead to a brutish and short trip indeed.

  • Know how to maintain and operate a firearm: Unless you plan on subsisting on plants and fungi, you will need to hunt mutants to meet your protein needs. The 7.62×51mm NATO is the bare minimum in stopping power; anything weaker is unlikely to pierce the reinforced hide of a medium- sized mutant.
  • Maintain trade relations with a settlement or colony: Scavenging for essentials, such as ammunition and medicine, in mutant-infested ruins is a dangerous activity. Even the most seasoned nomadic gangs keep a few trusted suppliers around to keep them stocked.
  • Work a trade: Traders do not part with their wares without receiving something of equal value in return. Make sure to not show up at a trading outpost empty-handed. Hides and trophies, exotic foods, and scavenged goods almost always attract buyers.
  • Cover your tracks: The ability to move quickly and stealthily is key to survival in the great outdoors. Mutants will happily make an easy meal out of a careless rookie who leaves a scent trail or trespasses into hunting grounds.
  • Know how to purify water: You can go weeks without food, but only days without water. Learn how to purify it or dig deep enough to find it.
  • Know how to find your way: GPS is among the many technologies that have been lost with the fall of civilized society. To get around, you will need to rely on landmarks, stars, and your intuition.
  • Light fires selectively: Think twice before making a fire to warm yourself; mutants can spot a flame from miles away. Only light a fire if you are ready to fight for it.
  • Learn how to find safe shelters: Avoid being ambushed while you are sleeping or recuperating! Study the fauna of the land and choose shelters they cannot reach or prefer to avoid.
