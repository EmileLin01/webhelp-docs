Jobs in a Post-Apocalyptic World

Examples of jobs in a post-apocalyptic world.

With life shifts back to a subsistence-based model, the vast majority of jobs have been eliminated, leaving only work directly tied to survival and the means of production. For instance, office workers fortunate enough to have survived the first months of the Voidborne virus outbreak have all retrained themselves to take on roles that contribute directly to their settlement or colony's resilience. Anyone wishing to earn a place in their community must demonstrate that they can perform these often manual (and thankless) jobs on a day-to-day basis.

Low-Risk

Teacher
Educates children or retrains adults in essential survival skills and essential work.
Water Purifier
Operates or maintains water purification systems.
Crafter
Fabricates tools, weapons, and equipment from salvaged parts and natural resources.
Engineer
Designs and builds vital machinery and buildings using scavenged parts and natural resources.
Radio Operator
Handles long-range communication via radio or signal systems.
Medic
Provides basic care using a combination of modern medicine and wild or farmed herbs.
Farmer
Grow produces and other plants in greenhouses.
Mechanic
Maintains and repairs vehicles like scavenged trucks, bikes, or makeshift transports.

Medium-Risk

Warden
Maintains order within settlements, dealing with internal disputes and theft.
Scout
Monitors the perimeter of a settlement or nearby regions for threats.
Miner
Mine ores, such as iron, copper, and tin from caves and quarries.
Steelworker
Operates furnaces to refine metal ore or scrap into usable steel.
Chemist
Works with cleaning agents, medicines, fuel, or explosives.
Trapmaker
Designs, builds, maintains, and sets traps to defend settlements from wildlife or intruders.
Combat Instructor
Instructs others in diverse combat techniques, including self-defense against mutants and hostile humans.
Woodcutter
Cuts and hauls wood for construction and fuel.

High-Risk

Hunter
Ventures into the surrounding wilds to capture smaller mutant animals as a source of protein.
Soldier
Engages mutants and hostile humans in combat when duty demands.
Messenger
Travels across vast distances to deliver communications to other settlements and colonies.
Scavenger
Scours ruins and mutant-infested areas to retrieve valuable supplies
Explorers
Surveys new lands and maps out territories for potential settlement.

This list of jobs is by no means comprehensive and does not account for niche or non-essential roles, such as restaurateurs, bartenders, and various types of entertainers.
